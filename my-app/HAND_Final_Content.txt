3) CLIMATE CHANGE 
Climate Change and Our Commitment to Eco-Friendly Leaf Plate Making
Climate change is one of the most pressing global challenges of our time. Rising temperatures, unpredictable weather patterns, deforestation, and increasing plastic pollution are all contributing to serious environmental damage. A major yet often overlooked cause of this problem is single-use plastic, especially plastic plates and disposable tableware that take hundreds of years to decompose.
The Impact of Plastic on Climate Change
Plastic production relies heavily on fossil fuels and releases large amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. After use, plastic plates often end up in landfills, oceans, or are openly burned-further increasing carbon emissions, harming wildlife, and polluting soil and water. This unsustainable cycle accelerates climate change and threatens the health of future generations.
Leaf Plates: A Sustainable Alternative
As part of our climate action initiatives, leaf plate making is one of our key eco-friendly projects. Leaf plates, made from naturally fallen leaves such as areca or sal leaves, are 100% biodegradable, compostable, and plastic-free. They decompose naturally without releasing toxic substances, making them an ideal alternative to disposable plastic plates.
Promoting Eco-Friendly Practices
By encouraging the use of eco-friendly leaf plates, we aim to:
* Reduce plastic waste and environmental pollution
* Lower carbon footprint and greenhouse gas emissions
* Promote sustainable livelihoods through leaf plate making projects
* Support circular economy and zero-waste practices
* Raise awareness about climate change and responsible consumption

Vistaraku Making Training (Leaf Plate Production Program)
Empowering Women | Promoting Green Livelihoods | Protecting the Environment
Our Vistaraku Making Training program supports rural women in gaining practical skills to produce eco-friendly leaf plates—locally known as Vistaraku—that are widely used for traditional functions, events, and sustainable food packaging. Through hands-on workshops, we train women in leaf collection, cleaning, processing, stitching, and plate finishing, ensuring high-quality and market-ready products.
This sustainable livelihood initiative helps women earn a stable income from resources available within their own communities, reducing dependency on single-use plastics and supporting environmental conservation. By creating a green income source, we build confidence, enhance economic resilience, and promote the use of biodegradable products that align with India’s growing demand for eco-conscious alternatives.
At HAND, we ensure continuous support through market linkages, micro-enterprise guidance, and collective production models, helping women establish small-scale, home-based businesses. This initiative not only advances women’s empowerment but also contributes to a cleaner ecosystem and stronger local economies.
When women thrive through eco-friendly enterprises, nature and communities grow together.

Adolescence Education
Adolescence is a sensitive stage of growing up, when young people face many emotional and social changes. At this age, they may feel pressure from friends or curiosity that can lead them toward harmful habits like drug or substance abuse. It is important to help students understand the dangers of drugs, how they affect the brain and body, and how to say “no” with confidence. Creating a safe space in schools for open conversations can protect children and guide them toward healthier choices.

STEM (AI & ROBOTICS)
To prepare children for a rapidly evolving world, HAND introduces hands-on STEM education, AI learning, and robotics training to students in rural and underserved communities. Our interactive learning model includes workshops, maker-spaces, coding sessions, and project-based activities that spark curiosity and innovation.
Through these future-focused programs, students build critical thinking, problem-solving skills, teamwork, creativity, and technological confidence — essential competencies for tomorrow’s digital job market. We ensure that children from marginalized backgrounds gain exposure to the same advanced learning opportunities available to students globally, helping them become future-ready, skilled, and competitive.
By integrating 21st-century skills, digital literacy, and real-world applications into education, we empower students to explore new career pathways in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, ultimately contributing to brighter futures for themselves and their communities.
